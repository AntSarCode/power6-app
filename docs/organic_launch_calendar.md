# Power6 30-Day Organic Launch Calendar

## Cadence
Post three times per week for four weeks. Keep each post useful on its own, then end with a light Power6 mention.

Channels:
- LinkedIn
- X
- Personal network
- Productivity communities where self-promotion is allowed

## Week 1 - Problem Awareness
| Day | Theme | Post Angle | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Endless lists | "What would change if your task list stopped at six?" | Ask readers to name their six for today. |
| 3 | Daily focus | Explain why fewer tasks can create better execution. | Invite people to try Power6. |
| 5 | Founder note | Share why Power6 is built around completion, not app opens. | Ask for beta feedback/testimonials. |

## Week 2 - Workflow Education
| Day | Theme | Post Angle | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 8 | Planning ritual | A 3-minute morning planning routine: choose, rank, start. | Ask what makes planning hard. |
| 10 | Review habit | Unfinished work should roll forward without guilt. | Invite replies from busy professionals. |
| 12 | Streaks | A productivity streak should reward completed work. | Ask users to share a completed Power6 day. |

## Week 3 - Social Proof
| Day | Theme | Post Angle | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 15 | Early quote | Share one early-user quote or lesson. | Ask for more testers. |
| 17 | Use case | Show how a freelancer/manager/student might use six slots. | Ask which use case fits readers. |
| 19 | Before/after | Endless list vs six-task plan. | Link to Power6/support page. |

## Week 4 - Conversion And Learning
| Day | Theme | Post Angle | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 22 | Pro insight | Explain how timeline/CSV helps review finished work. | Ask who reviews productivity weekly. |
| 24 | Objection | "What if I have more than six tasks?" Explain focus vs capture. | Invite comments. |
| 26 | Launch recap | Share what you learned from the first month. | Ask for reviews/testimonials. |

## Weekly Review
Every Friday, record:
- Installs
- Signups
- First task created
- Task completions
- Subscription screen views
- Checkout starts
- Best post by replies/clicks
- One thing to change next week
