# Power6 Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [04/18/2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how Power6 collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use the Power6 website, application, and related services.

By using Power6, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### A. Information You Provide

* account information such as name, email address, username, and password;
* profile or account preferences;
* tasks, entries, streak data, and other content you submit;
* support inquiries, feedback, or communications you send to us;
* billing-related information necessary to manage subscriptions.

### B. Information Collected Automatically

* device information;
* browser type and operating system;
* IP address and approximate location information;
* usage data, app interactions, and log information;
* cookies, local storage data, and similar technologies.

### C. Payment Information

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Power6 does not store full payment card numbers. We may receive limited subscription, billing, and transaction metadata from our payment processor.

## 2. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

* provide, operate, and maintain Power6;
* create and manage user accounts;
* process subscriptions and manage billing status;
* sync plan access and subscription tier changes;
* store and display your tasks, streaks, and timeline data;
* improve features, performance, usability, and reliability;
* provide customer support;
* detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents;
* comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## 3. How We Share Information

We may share information:

* with service providers that help operate the platform, such as hosting providers, analytics providers, database providers, and payment processors;
* when required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process;
* to protect rights, safety, security, or property;
* in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of the business.

We do not sell your personal information for money.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain legitimate business records.

If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or de-identify your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate operational purposes.

## 5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

You can control certain cookie settings through your browser, though disabling them may affect functionality.

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
