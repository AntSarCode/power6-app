# Power6 Support

Need help with Power6, subscriptions, account deletion, or App Store billing?

Email: support@power6.app

Power6 helps busy professionals choose the six tasks that matter today, complete them with focus, and build consistent momentum with review, streaks, badges, and Pro insights.

For account deletion, open Power6, sign in, then go to Menu > Account settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, tasks, subscription records, badges, and related Power6 data.

For Apple App Store subscriptions, use Restore Purchases on the subscription screen after reinstalling the app or moving to a new device.

Expected support response time: 1-3 business days.
