# Power6 Outreach And Testimonials Tracker

## Outreach Goal
Contact 20-30 busy professionals, freelancers, coaches, small business operators, and students during the first launch cycle.

## Message Template
Hi [Name], I launched Power6, a focused daily planning app built around choosing and completing six meaningful tasks. I am collecting feedback from people who manage a lot of moving pieces. Would you be open to trying it for a few days and sending one thing that felt useful and one thing that felt confusing?

## Follow-Up Template
Thanks again for trying Power6. If one sentence describes what felt useful, could I quote you on the app page or launch posts? I can use your first name only, or keep it anonymous.

## Testimonial Guidelines
- Ask for specific outcomes: focus, clarity, completion, less task-list overwhelm.
- Avoid medical, financial, or exaggerated productivity claims.
- Use short quotes that fit screenshots, landing copy, or social posts.
- Keep consent written in email, DM, or another saved channel.

## Useful Questions
- What was the first moment Power6 made sense?
- What felt confusing or unnecessary?
- Did the six-task limit feel helpful or restrictive?
- What would make you keep using it for seven days?
- Would any paid feature feel worth subscribing for?
