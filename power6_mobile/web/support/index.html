Power6 Support

Email support@power6.app for account help, subscription help, restore purchase help, account access, or account deletion.

Account help

Contact support if you cannot sign in, need help understanding your account state, or have questions about stored Power6 data.

Subscription help

For billing questions about iPhone or iPad subscriptions, manage your subscription through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Restore purchase help

On iPhone or iPad, open the subscription screen and tap Restore Purchases after reinstalling Power6 or moving to a new device.

Account deletion

In the Power6 app, sign in and open Menu > Account settings > Delete Account. Confirm the warning to permanently delete your account, tasks, subscription records, badges, and related Power6 data.

Response time

Support requests are typically answered within 1-3 business days.
